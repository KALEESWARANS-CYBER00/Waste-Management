Why Zero Waste?



         "Reducing waste is essential for protecting our planet  With millions of tons 
         of waste produced every year, adopting a zero waste lifestyle can significantly
         decrease our carbon footprint. Small actions, like using reusable bags or
         composting, can lead to substantial environmental benefits."


    
Getting Started

         
    
 1.Assess Your Waste: Take a week to track what you throw away.
 2.Choose Reusables: Replace single-use items with durable alternatives.
 3.Start Composting: Divert organic waste from landfills.
 4.Shop Smart: Bring your own containers to bulk stores."
