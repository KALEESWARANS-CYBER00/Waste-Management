Zero Waste India is a movement aimed at reducing the country's waste generation by promoting sustainable practices like recycling, composting, and reducing the use of single-use plastics. The goal is to minimize the amount of waste sent to landfills and incinerators, while encouraging people to adopt eco-friendly habits such as using reusable products, conserving resources, and supporting local, environmentally conscious businesses. By working together, individuals and communities in India can help create a cleaner, healthier environment for future generations.
